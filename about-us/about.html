Vision

MyStudyPal is a free study tool effective for helping students study smarter not harder. Contrary to popular belief that studying for long hours is ideal for A students, research has shown that studying in blocks is a more productive way of studying. And that is what MyStudyPal does for you!

Build a productive study habit that will fetch you those A's.
